KERALA   ARTICLE

    Kerala, located in southern India, is known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and progressive social development. With its picturesque beaches, serene backwaters, and lush hill stations like Munnar, the state is a top tourist destination. The Western Ghats offer rich biodiversity, while Keralaâ€™s backwaters, especially in Alleppey, provide unique houseboat experiences. Kerala boasts one of the highest literacy rates in India and is recognized for its excellent healthcare system. Culturally, it is famous for classical dance forms like **Kathakali** and vibrant festivals such as **Onam**. The state's cuisine, heavily influenced by rice, coconut, and spices, includes dishes like **fish curry**, **appam**, and the traditional **sadya**. Known as the cradle of **Ayurveda**, Kerala is also a hub for wellness tourism, offering Ayurvedic treatments in scenic surroundings. The state combines natural beauty with strong education, healthcare, and social equality, making it a unique destination in India.
